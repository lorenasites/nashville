🧳 Carry-On Only Packing Guide

International Flight Strategy (YYZ ✈️ BNA)

Pack light, stay comfortable, and leave empty space for souvenirs:

  • The "Leave Room to Shop" Golden Rule: Only pack your suitcase 60–70% full on the way there. You’ll want plenty of room to bring back clothing, gifts, and boutique finds from 12 South and Green Hills!
  • The Walkable Footwear Duo: Skip the heavy cowboy boots. Bring 2 pairs of your absolute most comfortable, broken-in walking sneakers or sandals. You'll be doing plenty of walking in Centennial Park and strolling down Broadway.
  • The Tourist Uniform: Light, breathable layers. Think classic denim shorts, airy tees, cute summer sundresses, and a light cardigan or denim jacket for chilly air-conditioned restaurants or evening pool nights.
  • Day Baggage: Pack a lightweight crossbody bag or a small backpack for daily exploring so your hands are free for shopping bags and holding iced coffees.
  • Liquid Reminder: Since it's carry-on only, keep all liquids, gels, and creams under 100ml (3.4 oz) and packed into a clear, one-quart zip-top bag.

🛍️ Seamless Stops Along Our Route

Fun, quick things to look out for right where you already plan to be:

While on Broadway (Sunday afternoon):
  • Ernest Tubb Record Shop — A legendary, historic stop to browse classic country music history right amidst the honky-tonks.
  • Legends Gift Shop — Perfect for picking up classic, fun Nashville tourist t-shirts, postcards, and memorabilia.
While in 12 South (Monday daytime):
  • The "I Believe in Nashville" Mural — Located right next to Edley's BBQ. It’s the ultimate classic tourist photo op!
  • Amelia's Flower Truck — Look out for the adorable vintage Volkswagen truck parked along the street filled with fresh blooms.
While in Green Hills (Right by the Hotel!):
  • Parnassus Books — A beautiful independent local bookstore co-owned by author Ann Patchett. It's a fantastic, quiet neighborhood gem to check out during your afternoon downtime.
